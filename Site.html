About

Hi, My name is Jane Stanton and I am in the process of learning code at The Starter League. I live in Chicago and am interested in learning as much as I can about becoming a front-end developer. I studied photography in college, where I took my first web design class. I currently am exploring many related components such as code, structure, typography, color, content and graphics. Most of my time is spent figuring out and creating pages with clean and efficient code.

When I am not practicing code, I work with high school students. I am a passionate runner and recently coached for a girls' cross country and track and field team. I also love photographing, volunteering at PAWS Chicago, and following music blogs.
